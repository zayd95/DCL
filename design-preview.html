Part 03

Typography Scale

⛔ Hard Rule — No Custom Font Sizes

text-[8px], text-[9px], text-[10px], text-[11px], text-[12px] are banned. They still exist in the codebase but must be migrated. Only these 6 tokens are allowed.

micro AU QUAI · LOT-BFF-01 · TRANSIT 9px / 900 Status chips, tight badges, timestamps
label INVENTAIRE · DÉPÔTS · VOIR TOUT 10px / 900 Section headers, tab labels, action links
caption SKU · DKR-BFF-01 · 28 500 kg total 11px / 700 Helper text, counters, sub-labels
body Poulet entier congelé — Lot SODIDA-06 13px / 500 Product names, form values, descriptions
title Viande Congelée 16px / 900 Card headings, modal titles, stock names
display 114 000 XOF 28px / 900 KPI values, hero numbers only
